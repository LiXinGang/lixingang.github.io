Shenlong Wang  

Email / CV / Git / Google Scholar

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto. My advisor is Raquel Urtasun. I am also part of Uber Advanced Technology Group Toronto and Vector Institute.

I am interested in computer vision, robotics and machine learning, specifically on topics such as 3D perception and reconstruction, localization and mapping, motion estimation, robot simulation and deep structured models. My recent work involves developing robust algorithms for self-driving and making autonomous vehicles more reliable and scalable.
